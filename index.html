About me

Seeking new opportunities in Data and AI/ML engineering

As a Data Professional, I am passionate about transforming raw data into actionable insights. With three years of experience, I am interested in designing and implementing data pipelines, building data models, and optimising data storage and retrieval for maximum efficiency.

My expertise lies in building and implementing efficient data pipelines, creating robust data models, and optimising data storage and retrieval processes to enhance organisational efficiency. I have a strong foundation in programming and databases, particularly in Python, R, and SQL, and am proficient in data analysis and visualisation using tools like Numpy, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, and Tableau, and my experience with machine learning frameworks like TensorFlow, Keras, Scikit-learn, and PyTorch. I am also skilled in Natural Language Processing, utilising NLTK, SpaCy, and other advanced techniques.

Beyond my technical prowess, I am a dedicated team player with excellent communication skills. My experience in collaborating with cross-functional teams and translating complex technical concepts into understandable terms has been a key aspect of my role. I aim to continue leveraging my skills and experiences in AI, machine learning, and data analysis to help organisations in their journey towards data-driven decision-making and achieving their strategic goals.
